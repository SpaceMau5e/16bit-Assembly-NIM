The Game Of Nim - README
Created by: Devin Kaltenbaugh

The Controls:
  W 	- moves the green cursor off of 'steal'
  A 	- moves the magenta cursor left
  S	- moves the green cursor onto 'steal'
  D 	- moves the magenta cursor right
  Enter - confirms the amount stolen when 'steal' is selected

Objective:
  Your objective is the take the star located on the right side of the
	game pieces window. You do this by taking 1, 2 or 3 pieces at
	a time.
